Twin Cities: The Jewel of Thessia

GM

Welcome

Inspired by Star Trek: TNG — The Outrageous Okona. The party arrives among the twin river cities of Ravenor (Patrician) and Vienetta (Mercantile), where a feud over the Jewel of Thessia and a whispered scandal draw bounty hunters like gulls to a trawler. The charming courier Okona has stepped on too many toes; both houses have posted alive‑only bounties. A ruthless Bounty Queen intends a very public capture at the Cattedrale dei Due Ponti.

Party
5 PCs • Tier 2 Level 3
Milestone: Level 4 on adventure completion
Mix Target
Combat 40% • Social 30% • Urban Explore 30%
Scaling to 4 PCs
Trim 20–25% BP; remove one body or hazard lane. Solo: Relentless 3→2 or reduce damage one die or remove one Engine lane.

The Setting

  • Ravenor — oaths and salons; dueling schools; House Bellavaro (motto: Virtù e Forma).
  • Vienetta — counting‑houses, docks, customs; House Marivale (motto: Apri la Via).
  • Twin links — the great bridge and ferry lanes; festival crowds provide cover and chaos.

The Threat

If the feud and hunter frenzy escalate, the bridge shuts, trade halts, and vigil justice spreads. A staged capture by the Bounty Queen could cement a false narrative and entrench the feud.

Global Clock
Bounty Heat (3–6 steps)

Rises with noise, failure, delay; increases rival hunter presence and replaces Rooftops with Warehouse when high.

Global Clock
Festival Eve (4 steps)

Markets → bridge fair → lantern night → public capture window at the Cathedral.

Recent Developments

  • Okona’s courier skiff was impounded; coded letters seized.
  • The Jeweler insists the Jewel is an heirloom reset; receipts seem to point both ways.
  • Midwife seen near Bellavaro estate at odd hours.
  • The Bounty Queen hired the Sibling Hunter Duo as advance team.
  • Ferry log shows a night crossing with a velvet case.
  • Oath‑court suggests a public reading—Queen wants that venue for a stunt.

Adventure Summary

  1. Arrive amid bounty buzz in Ravenor/Vienetta.
  2. Rescue/meet Okona on the run (Rooftops) or find him cornered (Warehouse) at higher Heat.
  3. Survive early hunter pressure at the docks; seize coded letters.
  4. Parlor social at House Bellavaro; pressure to make Okona marry Yanar.
  5. Bazaar mini‑boss to crack ciphers and gather provenance.
  6. Quiet river crossing; avoid patrols.
  7. Gallery social at House Marivale to confirm heirship & betrothal draft.
  8. Bridge mini‑boss (the Sibling Hunters) sent by the Queen.
  9. Finale Solo: Bounty Queen stages a public capture at the Cathedral; Social Interludes can void her contract and cue the proposal.

Adventure Hooks

  • The Richest Collar: both houses post rich alive‑only bounties for Okona.
  • The Rogue’s Bet: Okona offers a better payout if protected until the truth surfaces.
  • Keep the Bridge Open: Wardens quietly want the fair to run without curfews.

Adventure Beats

Each Beat includes a scene goal, environment, suggested adversaries, countdowns, outcomes, and 4‑PC trim. Numbers/stat blocks are T2‑L3 and will be finalized in the adversary pack.

Beat 1 — Whispers at Westgate (On‑Ramp) Type: Exploration → Social
Crowds press at the gate; hawkers cry festival deals. A Warden’s bell rings for order as a rumor passes from mouth to mouth—Okona, the rogue courier, has been seen on the rooftops.

Scene Goal: Learn feud basics; spot hunter activity; establish Bounty Heat.

Environment: (Event).

Adversaries: Informant Skulks; pickpocket Minions; Wardens (Standards) as pressure.

Countdown: “Rumor spreads” (3) → Heat +1.

Clue Drop: R1 fragment (pet‑names letter scrap + Okona’s sigil).

Outcomes: Leads to Rooftops (Beat 2) or Warehouse (Beat 2a) depending on Heat.

4‑PC Trim: remove a Warden Standard or convert two Minions to color.

Beat 2 — Rooftop Rendezvous (Meet Okona) Type: Exploration Skill → Social
Wind flutters laundry lines strung like pennants between slate roofs; a figure bounds ahead with a laugh and a backward bow.

Goal: Intercept Okona mid‑chase; decide to help or collar him.

Environment: (Traversal).

Adversaries: Rookie hunters (Minions); nimble Skulk chaser.

Countdown: “Line snaps” (3) → forced reposition; Heat +1 on failure.

Clue Drop: R4 (ferry chit) + R5 hint (“Queen never forgets a debt”).

4‑PC Trim: −2 Minions; keep one Skulk.

Beat 2a — Warehouse Standoff (Heat ≥ 2) Type: Combat + Exploration
Stacks of crates loom; a lantern clatters, oil fanning across the floor. Above, a skylight gapes—someone laughs in the rafters.

Goal: PCs find Okona cornered by dockside thugs; break the net.

Environment: (Event/Traversal).

Adversaries: Dock Thug Minions (Horde step); Bruiser Foreman (hook & chain); Skulk lookout.

Countdown: “Ignite” (oil slick) → burning lane hazard.

Clue Drop: Smeared signet wax + pawn/ledger stub to Marivale counting‑house.

4‑PC Trim: remove the Skulk and reduce Horde by 2.

Beat 3 — Dockside Debacle (First Dust‑Up) Type: Combat

Environment: (Event).

Adversaries: Leader (Bounty Factor), 2 Standards, 4 Minions; optional Skulk on a crane.

Countdown: “Crane jams” (3) → hazard lane locks on.

Clue Drop: R1 cipher key fragment from seized satchel.

4‑PC Trim: remove 1 Standard or 2 Minions and drop the Skulk.

Beat 4 — House of Vows (Bellavaro Parlor) Type: Social

Environment: (Social).

Focus: Pressure to make Okona marry Yanar Bellavaro; R2 pregnancy indicators.

Notes: Guards loom off‑screen; oathflame flickers on lies (Insight advantage; Stress on egregious deceit).

Beat 5 — Bazaar of Blades (Mini‑Boss: Boss + Engine) Type: Combat + Exploration

Environment: (Engine).

Adversaries: Leader (Guild Factor) + Engine; 2 Skulks; 4 Minions.

Countdown: “Guild whistles” (4) → one add wave.

Clue Drop: Decoded R1 (proposal wording) + R3 hint (jeweler ledger stub).

4‑PC Trim: remove 1 Skulk; cap add wave to once.

Beat 6 — River of Secrets (Quiet Crossing) Type: Exploration

Environment: (Traversal).

Focus: Slip to Vienetta without arrest; patrols are hazards more than foes.

Clue Drop: R4 corroboration from patrol logs.

Beat 7 — Sun Court Gallery (Marivale Social) Type: Social

Environment: (Social).

Clue Drop: R3 (signet seal + bond ledger proving heirloom reset) + R6 (betrothal draft).

Beat 8 — Bridge at Midnight (Mini‑Boss: Duo/Co‑Boss) Type: Combat

Environment: (Event).

Adversaries: Co‑Boss Duo (two elite Standards with synergy), 2 Standards, 2 Minions.

Countdown: “Lanterns crash” (3) → burning slick zones.

Clue Drop: R5 (Queen’s personal contract addendum; Okona alive, unmaimed).

4‑PC Trim: remove 1 support Standard and 2 Minions or weaken one Co‑Boss reaction.

Beat 9 — Queen’s Net (Finale SOLO) Type: Combat + Social Interludes

Venue: Cattedrale dei Due Ponti — nave aisles, choir lofts, candelabras, processional ropes.

Solo: (Relentless 3; whip/net/rapier; Momentum on restrains).

Engines: Net‑launchers, shock bolas, crowd surges; bell towers cue hazard pulses. Countdown: “Crowd Panic” (4) → hazardous pew aisles.

Social Interludes (1/round): Present R‑clues to sway the crowd & Wardens. Each success disables an Engine lane or strips a Queen reaction; three successes void the public contract and force withdrawal/arrest. Cue Okona’s speech → proposal.

4‑PC Trim: Relentless 3→2 or −1 die damage or remove one Engine lane.

Conclusion

  • Public Exoneration: Contract voided; lovers recognized; swift private wedding; PCs gain city favor.
  • Quiet Justice: Families keep truth discreet; PCs paid to hush.
  • Fractured Peace: Queen escapes, swears revenge; river‑heist sequel.

Rewards & Loot

  • Writ of Free Passage: official letter granting toll‑free transit and priority moorage in Ravenor & Vienetta.
  • River Wardens’ Silver Brooch: marks PCs as deputies; advantage on checks with Wardens once/beat.
  • Note: The Jewel of Thessia is a Marivale ring given to Yanar—a story artifact, not a PC reward.
